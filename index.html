Yozu — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Summary: Yozu is an offline text converter for the Uzbek language. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The app uses Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect certain data as described below.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yozu ("we", "our", "the app"). Yozu is a mobile application that converts Uzbek text between Latin and Cyrillic scripts in real time. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

By using Yozu, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Yozu is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Text you enter for conversion
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Yozu stores the following data locally on your device only using SharedPreferences:

  • Conversion history — your recent text conversions (up to 100 entries)
  • Favorites — conversions you have marked as favorites
  • App settings — theme preference (light/dark/system) and clipboard auto-detect setting

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear your history at any time from within the app.

2.3 Information Collected by Third Parties

Yozu uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information as described in section 4.

3. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your conversion history and favorites
  • Remember your app preferences (theme, settings)
  • Improve your user experience within the app

4. Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

Yozu displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and process certain data in accordance with Google's privacy practices, including:

Data Type Purpose
Advertising ID To serve and personalize ads
Device information Device model, OS version, screen size
IP address Approximate location for relevant ads
Ad interaction data Clicks, views, and impressions

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. For more information, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & Data Collection

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

5. Ad Formats Used

Yozu uses the following ad formats:

  • Banner ads — small ads displayed at the bottom of the screen
  • App open ads — displayed when the app is opened or resumed
  • Rewarded ads — optional full-screen ads that users can choose to watch

6. Children's Privacy

Yozu is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

All app data (history, favorites, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted over the internet except through Google AdMob's standard ad serving process. We do not operate any servers or databases that store user data.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Conversion history — can be cleared within the app at any time
  • Favorites — can be removed individually within the app
  • All app data — is completely removed when you uninstall the app

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (visible within the app)
  • Delete your data (via app settings or by uninstalling)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (via device settings)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
